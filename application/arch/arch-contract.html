Advisors

I accept the invitation to participate in the Advanced Research and Creativity in Honors Program at the FIU Honors College as a Faculty Research Advisor. I understand that the purpose of the program is to prepare students for graduate study through active participation in undergraduate research and I subscribe to that purpose.

I understand that my acceptance commits me to the following responsibilities:

  1. Serve as an advisor and mentor to the student, guiding the identification, design, and completion of appropriate research resulting in a thesis or its equivalent by the end of the academic year.
  2. Give constructive and critical review of the student’s work, including a research proposal, abstract, academic poster, oral presentation, thesis or its creative equivalent.
  3. Abide by program guidelines and deadlines.
  4. Assess the student’s progress in relation to their research on a regular basis.
  5. Submit a letter grade for the student at the end of each semester through Panthersoft.
  6. ist the student in preparing proposals for appropriate presentation of research work at conferences, workshops and other venues for academic exchange.
  7. Assist in identifying potential graduate schools for the student.
  8. Assist student in the development of a personal statement and/or letter of application.
  9. Provide regular progress reports on the work submitted by the student.
  10. Communicate any questions or problems to the ARCH office.
  11. Thoroughly review and approve all work submitted to the ARCH office through the approval form that will be sent to you through FIU email. Please note the student will not receive credit for his/her work without this approval. The student will also not receive credit for his/her participation grade from the Honors College.
  12. Attend the ARCH Conference and, more specifically, the student's oral and poster presentation.
